SPECIALTY SHAVES

THE GENTLEMAN SHAVE N1000

This relaxing escape begins with a hot towel. Your skin is then prepared with a protective layer of Pre-Shave Oil and slathered with a hot Shaving Cream. Our skilled Master Barbers use a traditional Straight Razor with precision & mastery to shave with the grain and then finish with an Essential Oil which provides a soothing & relaxing experience. It concludes with the Aftershave Balm to hydrate this freshly-shaved skin.​ Please allow 30 minutes for this service.

THE ROYAL SHAVE N2000

Our signature service, the Royal Shave, is the ultimate indulgence. Experience this ultimate shave performed by our expertly-trained Master Barbers. This relaxing escape begins with a hot towel and a protective layer of pre-shave oil to prepare your skin. Next, shaving cream is applied to the face in a rich, hot lather. Our skilled Master Barbers use a traditional Straight Razor with precision & mastery to shave with the grain first, and then re-lather & shave once more against the grain for closeness. A second hot towel, infused with Essential Oil, replenishing the skin's moisture after the shave. Next, a cold towel infused with Essential Oil provides a smooth and relaxing experience. This Royal Shave concludes with the hydrating toner and the application of the Aftershave Balm to hydrate the freshly-shaven skin.​ Please allow 45 minutes for this service. *Prices subject to change without notice.​
