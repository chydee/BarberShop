ABOUT US

JUST CUT BARBER SHOP AND SHAVE LOUNGE

JUST CUT Barber Shop was established few years ago with the single mission to serve the gentleman client by offering only the best techniques, service and products available. Today, JUST CUT’s Barber Shop is widely recognized as the barbershop of choice in the Africa, and continues to set the highest standards of excellence in men’s haircutting, styling and grooming. Founded by Michael Alayande in 2010, JUST CUT’s Barber Shop believes in the art and craft of men’s grooming and exceptional, personalized service. Designed to be a welcoming environment where men can feel comfortable, JUST CUT’s Barber Shop offers classic, old-school haircutting and grooming techniques blended with today’s styles and trends. It’s a place where you are warmly greeted – by name – and where you will be well taken care of. Once seated in one of the handsome barber chairs, you can rest easy knowing you will receive your complete service in one spot – no ushering from place to place. We want you to kick back, relax and enjoy yourself. With two locations (Lagos and South Africa), JUST CUT’s Barber Shop offers a full-range of men’s grooming services from signature haircuts to straight razor shaves to color services.
